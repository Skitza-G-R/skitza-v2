WHAT CHANGED FROM V1

  • One focal item. The home picks the single most-important thing (new mix, or unpaid invoice, or upcoming session) and shows it big at the top. Everything else is a one-line row below.
  • No bento. Just a centered column, even on desktop. Same column on mobile. Same code path.
  • Studio chips → moved. Out of the home. Shown as "3 studios · Gili · Mor · Yuval" at the bottom. Tap takes you to the full list.
  • Balance → folded in. If you owe money, it appears as a row in "Also waiting" ("Pay ₪10,000"). No separate card.
  • Activity → moved. The activity feed gets its own page (link from the More tab). Home doesn't need it.
  • No "URGENT (2)" header. A small amber dot is enough. No shouting at the user.
  • Sentence-case labels. "Also waiting" — not "ALSO WAITING" or eyebrow-spammed everywhere.
  • The empty state is calm. If there's nothing to do, the page says "All quiet" and shows one button. We don't manufacture activity.

Inspired by

Linear's inbox (list rows, no cards) · Spotify's Listen Now (one big focal item) · Cal.com (your next thing front and center) · Things 3 (quiet typographic labels) · iMessage and Notion home (conversational, no badges everywhere).
